About Jim Turin & Sons

Jim Turin & Sons, Inc. is a family owned Oregon corporation headquartered in Sandy, Oregon. The company has excelled in the asphalt paving business since 1973. The Jim Turin & Sons’ team is exceptional when it comes to project experience. Ninety-five percent of their present work force has been with the company for over 10 years.
