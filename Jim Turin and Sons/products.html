Available Products

Our crushed aggregate is mined directly from our basalt quarry site located in Brightwood, Oregon. Since we are a quality local rock source you get not only high quality crushed rock, but also help contribute to the local economy.

  • PitRun

    Common uses: This is high quality clean basalt material with approximate sizes 0” – 12”. Maximum sizes may vary. Used for road stabilization, water outfalls and erosion protection.

  • RoadPack

    Common uses: This is a ¾”-0 screened material commonly used as an inexpensive backfill and can be used for top dressing on a gravel road.

  • Aggregate

    Common uses: This is an open graded aggregate typically used in the production of Hot Asphalt Concrete Pavement. Due to the free draining characteristics of this material, it is suitable for landscaping applications, water filtration, etc. It does not compact well.

  • Boulders

    Common uses: This material, sold in various sizes from two foot to four foot is typically used in landscaping, retaining walls and flood control.
